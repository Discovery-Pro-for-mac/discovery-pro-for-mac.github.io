Discovery Pro 8.18

Virtual analog synthesizer with 12 oscillators, sampler, and PADSynth. 4 layers, WAV/SF2 support, 2X–16X oversampling, Nord Lead 2 SysEx import/export. Arpeggiator, 12 filter types, stereo delay, gate effects. Unmatched control for music production.

Dual-Core Voice Processing

Voices split across two CPU cores in parallel. Doubles polyphony on heavy patches before dropouts.

Reworked MIDI Learn

Move knob, then controller—assignment made instantly. LCD guides with clear messages. Layer LEDs blink and confirm. Cancel anytime. Existing mappings stay active.

MIDI Board Management

Save, rename, delete, import/export controller mappings. Auto-grouped by manufacturer. 25 new boards: Arturia PolyBrute, Moog Subsequent, Sequential Prophet, and more.

Zero Delay Nordic Filter

High-precision NL2-flavored zero-delay-feedback filter. Joins Classic and Zero Delay models.

DiscoLink MIDI Out

Shared-memory MIDI bridge auto-connects receivers. Passthrough, NL2 Translate modes. Forwards notes, CCs, SysEx dumps. Per-layer channel routing.

How It Works

1. Load and Layer

Import WAV/SF2 samples or use 12 VA oscillators across 4 layers. Enable PADSynth for re-synthesis.

2. Shape Your Sound

Select from 12 filters (Nord Lead 2, Moog). Apply oversampling, panning modulation, stereo delay, gate.

3. Assign Controls

Enter MIDI Learn: twist knob, move controller. LCD confirms. Manage boards via import/export.

4. Connect and Output

Activate DiscoLink for MIDI bridging. Export SysEx or route layers to channels 1–4.
